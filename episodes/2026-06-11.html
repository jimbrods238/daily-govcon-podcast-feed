    # The Sole-Source AI Reflex — and Who It Locks Out


    The federal government is generating AI-related mission requirements faster than it is building the acquisition infrastructure to fill them competitively. Today's ICE sole-source to TRM Labs — up to $100 million, FFP, justified under Revolutionary FAR Overhaul provisions — is the clearest single expression of that gap, and a template agencies will repeat. Small AI shops not already in pre-solicitation conversations are structurally invisible to the awards that matter most right now.
