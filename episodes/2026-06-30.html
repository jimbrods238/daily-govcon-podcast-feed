    # FedRAMP 20x Drops the Drawbridge


    FedRAMP's finalized 2026 consolidated rules make 20x certification broadly available as of July 4th and mandatory by January 1st, 2027 — the single most consequential near-term authorization change for small AI-as-a-service firms selling to DoD and VA. But the civilian authorization wall coming down doesn't move DISA's IL-4 and IL-5 requirements, meaning DoD's internal ATO bottleneck survives intact. Today's episode closes the compliance-as-moat arc tracked all week and maps the trifecta — FedRAMP 20x, the OMB PQC mandate, and operational AI fielding — into a coherent near-term procurement picture for FFP-preferring AI shops.
